## About the Practice

Bloom Reimagined is a transition coaching practice founded by Yvonne Padilla — retired educator, school principal, and Master's-level leader. The practice is built on the belief that transformation isn't about returning to who you were. It's about reimagining who you're becoming.

Coaching is grounded in the proprietary **BLOOM Framework** (Believe, Listen, Observe, Own, Move) and anchored in **Kintsugi philosophy** — the Japanese art of repairing what's broken with gold, finding beauty and strength in the cracks rather than hiding them.

Sessions are direct, warm, and honest. No cheerleading. No empty validation. Just the kind of truth-telling that actually moves you forward.

Yvonne's approach draws from the research of Angela Duckworth, James Clear, Brené Brown, Stephen Covey, Francine Shapiro, and the science of neuroplasticity — tools that honor both the complexity of real life and the capacity every woman has to rewrite her story.

Bloom Reimagined serves women defined not by age or category, but by their moment — anyone standing at a crossroads and ready to stop measuring themselves against a bloom that was never meant to be theirs.

## About This Site

This is a fully custom-built static website — no templates, no WordPress, no page builders. Every line of HTML and CSS was written by hand to reflect the warmth, intentionality, and visual identity of the Bloom Reimagined brand.

### Features
- Fully responsive design (mobile, tablet, desktop)
- Smooth scroll navigation
- Animated section reveals on scroll
- Custom color palette derived from brand logo
- Professional contact form via Formspree (no backend required)
- Open Graph and Twitter Card meta tags for social sharing
- SEO-optimized meta descriptions and canonical URL
- Hosted on GitHub Pages with custom domain

### Tech Stack
- **HTML5** — semantic, accessible markup
- **CSS3** — custom properties, flexbox, grid, animations
- **JavaScript** — scroll behavior, intersection observer, mobile nav
- **Formspree** — professional contact form handling
- **Google Fonts** — Cormorant Garamond + Nunito
- **GitHub Pages** — free hosting with custom domain
- **GoDaddy DNS** — custom domain routing

### Design Decisions
- **Cormorant Garamond** for display headings — elegant, editorial, feminine without being delicate
- **Nunito** for body text — warm, readable, approachable
- Color palette of cream, sage, terracotta, and warm brown — grounded in nature, aligned with the Kintsugi philosophy of the brand
- No stock photography — the design relies on typography, color, and whitespace to carry the brand
